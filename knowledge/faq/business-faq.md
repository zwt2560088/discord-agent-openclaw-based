# Business FAQ

## Business Operations Related Questions

### 1. Business Model and Pricing
**Q: What is your business model?**
A: We use a hybrid business model:
- **B2C Service**: Direct boosting and mods for players
- **B2B Partnership**: Cooperation with game studios and esports teams
- **Platform Service**: G2G, U7buy platform store operations
- **Technical Service**: Technical solutions for other boosting providers

**Q: How is pricing determined?**
A: Pricing strategy:
- **Base Pricing**: Market research + cost calculation
- **Dynamic Adjustment**: Adjust based on demand, season, events
- **Member Discount**: VIP customers enjoy discounts
- **Bulk Discount**: Special pricing for large orders

### 2. Service Quality and Guarantee
**Q: How do you ensure service quality?**
A: Quality assurance system:
- **Three-Level Review**: Order review, process monitoring, delivery check
- **Customer Rating**: Customer rates after each order completion
- **Booster Rating**: Dynamic rating adjustment based on performance
- **Refund Guarantee**: Full refund if not satisfied

**Q: Is there after-sales service?**
A: Complete after-sales support:
- **7-Day Warranty**: Free maintenance for all services within 7 days
- **Technical Support**: 24-hour technical response
- **Problem Resolution**: Professional team handles complaints
- **Continuous Improvement**: Optimize service based on feedback

### 4. Partnership and Expansion
