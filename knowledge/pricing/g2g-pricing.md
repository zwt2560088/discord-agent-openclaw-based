# Platform Pricing Strategy

## 💬 Discord Platform Pricing

### Pricing Strategy
- **Direct Customers**: Lowest price, highest profit margin
- **Member Discount**: Member discounts and points
- **Bulk Discount**: Discount for multiple orders

### Specific Pricing
- **Boosting Services**: 90% of standard price (85% for members)
- **Mods**: 95% of standard price (90% for members)
- **Package Services**: Extra 10% discount

### Promotions
- **New Customer First Order**: 20% off
- **Referral Rebate**: 10% rebate for referring new customers
- **Holiday Events**: Extra discounts on specific holidays

## 🛒 G2G Platform Pricing

### Pricing Strategy
- **Platform Commission**: Consider G2G platform commission (~10%)
- **Competitive Pricing**: Reference competitor prices
- **Sales-Oriented**: Lower prices appropriately to increase sales

### Specific Pricing
- **Boosting Services**: 120% of standard price
- **Mods**: 125% of standard price
- **Service Fee**: Includes platform handling fee

### Optimization Strategy
- **Title Optimization**: Use hot keywords
- **Detailed Description**: Detailed service content
- **Fast Response**: Improve response speed and rating

## 💡 Price Adjustment Strategy

### Dynamic Pricing
- **Peak Season**: Appropriate price increase when new game version releases
- **Off Season**: Provide discounts during game off-season
- **Competition**: Adjust prices based on competitors

### Membership System
- **Tier System**: Member tiers based on spending amount
- **Tier Benefits**: Different discounts for different tiers
- **Points System**: Earn points from spending, points as cash
